About us


We’ve made it! We’ve arrived! We’re here! The Primi Piati bridges the traditional with the contemporary, bringing live-fire cooking, housemade pasta, and a robust Italian wine list to the heart of Ohio. The Primi Piati is fashioned after the green rooms in theaters and studios where performers relax when they are not on stage or camera. Everyone is a star at The Primi Piati with our immersive cocktail experiences, VIP service and the best views of the city.
